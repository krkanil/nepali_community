About Us

Welcome to the Nepali Samaj in Fort Wayne, IN. We are a vibrant community dedicated to preserving and promoting Nepali culture and traditions. Our community is a melting pot of diverse individuals who share a common heritage and a passion for our rich cultural legacy. We strive to create a welcoming environment where everyone feels at home, regardless of their background or how long they have been part of the community.

Our mission is to foster a sense of unity and belonging among Nepali individuals and families in Fort Wayne. We aim to provide a platform for cultural exchange, support, and growth. Through various events, programs, and initiatives, we celebrate our traditions, language, and customs, ensuring they are passed down to future generations.

We organize a wide range of activities throughout the year, including cultural festivals, educational workshops, social gatherings, and community service projects. These events not only help us stay connected to our roots but also provide opportunities for personal and professional development. We believe in the power of community and the importance of coming together to support one another.

We are proud of our heritage and the contributions our community has made to the broader Fort Wayne area. Our members are actively involved in various local initiatives, and we strive to build strong relationships with other community organizations. By working together, we can create a more inclusive and vibrant community for all.

We invite you to join us and be a part of our journey. Whether you are looking to connect with your roots, learn more about Nepali culture, or simply meet new people, there is a place for you in our community. Together, we can celebrate our heritage, support one another, and build a brighter future for generations to come.
